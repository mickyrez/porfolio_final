About Me

My name is Michael Gutierrez and I am a Web Developer. Ever since I can remember I have been interested in computers and what they are capable of. I am always striving to push the boundaries and Web Development is the perfect environment to do so.
It all started when I was introduced to a trial version of Adobe Dreamweaver when I was 10 years old. I was fascinated by hyperlinks and the fact that you could display information in so many different and interactive ways.


When I made my first website I was so excited that I showed everyone that I knew and the positive reinforcement I received then led me to persue my passion.
I made several web sites in my free time throughout highschool but I spent most of my time with photo manipulation in Adobe Photoshop and dabbled in some animation.


Upon completing the HSC, while everyone else was still confused about their ambitions for the future, I was wholehearted in my decision to continue my studies in Web Development and obtain a prospective career in this field.

I originally anticipated studying at the University of Wollongong, but as they did not have a course specific to my interests, I decided to do a Diploma of Information Technology (Multimedia) at Wollongong TAFE.
I felt this course was more suitable to the field of IT that I wished to explore, so I pressed through the 18 month course in only one year, and luckily by then, the University of Wollongong had just released a Web Design and Development Major for the Bachelor of Information Technology course, which I have completed.

I am concerned with the balance between design that is beautiful, design that is functional, and design that is intelligent. I challenge myself to achieve the finest relation between those three in every project. Work that embodies all of these qualities sensibly can be enjoyed timelessly.
